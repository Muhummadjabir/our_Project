Mudos Pharmaceutical

Mudos Pharmaceutical (Nigeria) Limited with offices in Minna, Niger State and Asaba, Delta State, is a leading healthcare, medical equipment and consumer care company in Nigeria. Our company is committed to meeting the growing needs of healthcare and pharmaceutical distribution in the country through various agreements and joint ventures with many of the world’s leading Pharmaceutical companies. Mudos Pharmaceutical has a unique distribution and marketing network of more than 20 sales and medical representatives (with a good number of pharmacists). As a wholesale organization giving a chain of local pharmacy store, the company is managed by a highly experienced team consisting of Nigerians with diverse backgrounds and skill sets
